Winston Churchill once said:

"The further you look into the past, the further you can see into the future"

The Past and the Futures are a reflection of life and its cycle. We can look into the past, but we cannot change it, we certainly cannot predict the future, but if we take intelligently calculated steps today, we can try to bring positive results in our future and in our life. Just as the human life, stocks have a life too, you can peek at their past, deep enough and stare at the present without blinking your eyes and foresee the future, some of the great men of our time or before we have proved, it is possible. You may or may not be that man, we certainly cannot claim to be either, but the good thing is, the Wisdom of these great men is still around, they have left many words of wisdom for us, to learn and walk in their footsteps.

The world of Stocks and Shares have many uneven and unseen surfaces, unless we follow the people, who walked on these surfaces before us and reached their goals, we might get lost in a puzzle and do not know where to go. A Step taken in the right direction today will protect your future, and tomorrow, your past would be shining like armor, but a wrong decision today will not only bring the uncertainty in your future, but your past will not be glorious.

If you have decided to explore this arena, you should be well equipped to tackle the most difficult job in the world, to control your emotions, the life which leads towards the wall street has no room for emotions.

Stock Valuation

Valuing a stock requires not just an estimate its fair value, but also to determine its potential price range, considering market behavior aspects. One of the behavioral valuation tools is the stock image, a coefficient that bridges the theoretical fair value and the market price.
