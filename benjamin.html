Who was Benjamin Graham and why should you listen to him?
Benjamin Graham was not only, the greatest investor who ever lived, he was also the greatest practical thinker of all times, his philosophy changed the way the wall street works today. A Mentor, A pattern and a Legend.
Benjamin Graham has outlined the ground principal for the Layman investor, even though some of the prominent financial analyst, who at one time or another were responsible of running the biggest and largest hedge funds on the Wall Street, followed his advice and learned from his wisdom. Warren Buffet is the greatest example of all, his endeavor is ex.
