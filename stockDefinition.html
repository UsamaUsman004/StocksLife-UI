Stock Definition

In a simple version, a share in stock, represents the legal ownership in a business. Companies issue stock, which are then trade in different sectors of Stock Exchanges to the public.

Why the stocks are created. Some common features:

1. One of the reasons, is to allow the public to buy stock of a company, to raise capital (money) to implement workable ideas and to extend business operation, such as opening multiple locations or starting overseas operation or for general improvement.

2. On the other hand, these options to buy Stocks in successful companies lends an opportunity for the public to invest and earn satisfactory returns on their capital to achieve their financial goals.

3.Buying stocks also provide an opportunity to those who do not have the time or expertise to operate or supervise an enterprise or act as entrepreneur, this provides a clear chance to be part of an organization of their choice an interest and make money at the same time.
