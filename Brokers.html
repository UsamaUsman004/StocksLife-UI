Brokers

It is very vital to choose the best stock broker, who not only understand the need of each individual but also be able to serve its investors with custom made trades as per its client’s requirement and expectations.

It is extremely important for an individual investor to do his due diligence to expect a profitable proposition from his broker. A good broker is like a good financial adviser who always protects his client’s investment.

Generally, less profitable trades are results of lack-of-research and in the absence of simple trading platforms. An investor should consider the following before deciding to start trading through a reputable trade house.

It is always important to choose a well-known, established broker for your portfolio.

How much it cost to buy shares or stock?

Is the brokerage house charges fees based on the order type or order size?

Always try to find brokers who offer flat-fee trades, meaning charging a flat rate regardless the type of buying order.

Customer Service is an important tool offered by the brokerage houses, generally a designated adviser is assigned to each individual investor, who is professionally trained to provide professional and neutral advice, based on client’s requirement and is also available to serve its clients. It is very important for some brokerage house, as to how to treat their customers.

Trading is much easier when you have brilliantly tested tools at your fingertips. Generally, all the excellent brokers provide well tested tools to their clients, to choose the nature of their trade based on skillfully tailored trades, pretty much all these trade related websites relay live streaming of trading platforms, from different Stock Exchanges, these tools also provide a real time learning experience and makes it a lot easier for general investor.

It is the policy of some brokers to offer new accounts with no minimum deposit requirement, which is quite simple and easier for first time investor, who are not ready to invest a size able amount of money without learning the trade.

They can start with a bare minimum and increase their portfolio as they learn the trade. While some highly established and reputable brokers only offer to open new accounts with several thousand dollars to start with.
