Warren Buffet

Listed here, are all the key words, when it comes to stock market and the selection of stock. The life at Wall street revolves around these words. Either you use all these features from within you or you seek advice from whomever you think has better knowledge than you do! Before you attempt to use your intelligence or the expertise of the genius at Wall Street.

Reveal the wisdom of Warren Buffet Below.

  • Business Opportunity
    Opportunities come infrequently. When it rains gold, put out the bucket, not the thimble.” Warren Buffett
  • Business Philosophy
    Rule No. 1: Never lose money.
    Rule No. 2: Never forget rule number 1. -Warren Buffett.
  • Business Science
    Rule No. 1: Never lose money.
    Rule No. 2: Never forget rule number 1. -Warren Buffett.
  • Wisdom
    Warren buffet on wisdom: The stock market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient. warren buffet.
    Warren buffet on wisdom: I will tell you how to become rich. Close the doors. Be fearful when others are greedy. Be greedy when others are fearful.”
    Warren buffet on business wisdom: Wall Street is the only place that people ride in a Rolls Royce to get advice from those who take the subway.”
  • Knowledge
    Risk comes from not knowing what you are doing.
  • Discipline
    “We don’t have to be smarter than the rest. We have to be more disciplined than the rest.”
  • Management
    When we own portions of outstanding businesses with outstanding managements, our favorite holding period is forever.
  • Confidence
    I never attempt to make money on the stock market. I buy on the assumption that they could close the market the next day and reopen it for ten years
  • Emotions
    “Unless you can watch your stock holding decline by 50% without becoming panic-stricken, you should not be in the stock market.”
  • Forecasting
    Forecasts may tell you a great deal about the forecaster; they tell you nothing about the future.
  • Value
    “Price is what you pay; value is what you get.’ Whether we’re talking about socks or stocks, I like buying quality merchandise when it is marked down.”
