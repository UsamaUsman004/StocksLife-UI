What We Do?

Through Stocks Life, we have highlighted the information of the most popular sectors/industries in United States and the companies under all these categories such Health Care and IT.

All these companies are listed under different stock Exchanges in United States.

Then for each company, we have put together a detailed work sheet. Everything you need to know about a particular company, such as Apple or Microsoft, before making a decision, either to invest or walk away, is available in these work sheets.
The life cycle of the company.
The stock trading history for many years.
The Financial Data.
The Live Feed from the Stock Exchange for that company; and The Stock analyses.

We have also added the details of companies which are regarded as blue chip companies and the companies who pays regular dividends and much more. It’s all here at your fingertips. To further assist you, we have added expert views.

These are vital and fruitful advises from the prominent investors and mentors of our generation and the generations before us, Such as, the Great “Benjamin Graham” and Our own “Warren Buffet” besides others. Read and learn from the Masters. There is a detailed list of brokers with active links and there is commentary from the experts, the criteria as to how, choose a good broker and what to expect and be ready.

We have also added a variety of use full links to help you dig further and deeper and explore.

Finally, there is a member zone where you will find pretty interesting information from other members, their success stories and much more.
